The Author

Written With Love for Tobago

Born and raised in Tobago, the author grew up surrounded by the stories, traditions, and natural beauty that fill these pages. This book is a labour of love — a way to preserve and share the cultural treasures that make Tobago truly special.

Drawing from years of research, personal experience, and conversations with elders and cultural keepers across the island, I Am Tobago is both a celebration and a preservation of heritage that might otherwise be lost to time.
